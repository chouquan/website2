Making London the world’s best big city for cycling ！

The Mayor's Transport Strategy, published in March 2018, sets out a vision for a fairer, greener, healthier and more prosperous London. This includes making cycling appealing to many more people, and ensuring that London's streets are safe and accessible for everyone who wants to cycle.



The cycling boom under the coronavirus pandemic !

Data released by Transport for London shows that since the began of the coronavirus pandemic, there has been a significant increase in walking and cycling activities in the UK. After the first national lockdown in March 2020, people's travel habits have changed significantly, from 29 percent between January and March 2020 to an estimated 46 percent between April to June 2020 (Travel in London 2020).
