How safe is cycling in London?

We hope to answer four questions:

  • Where did the cycling accident happen?

  • Where are the infrastructures that help improve cycling safety

  • What are the characteristics of the spatial distribution of cycling infrastructures and cycling accident

  • How should each district adjust its policies to provide a safer riding environment?

Spatial Scale and Data Source

This research focused on the middle layer super output area(MSOA) of London. （983个区） The MSOA was created by the Office for National Statistics and used to explore the small areas of the city. It can make it easier for us to find problems in a smaller area.
