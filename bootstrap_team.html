Team

We are a group of urban data analysts from the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis of University College London. We have never met because of the coronavirus pandemic. But this does not hinder the close communication between us , and our pursuit and exploration of urban data science. We hope that this research will help realize London’s vision of the world’s best big city for cycling. We also hope that of the coronavirus pandemic will end as soon as possible and that we can meet in London and start a green and free journey.

Looking forward to meeting in London for cycling  ！
